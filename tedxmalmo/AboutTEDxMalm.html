TEDx

TEDx is a grassroots initiative, created in the spirit of TED’s overall mission to research and discover “ideas worth spreading.” TEDx brings the spirit of TED to local communities around the globe through TEDx events. These events are organized by passionate individuals who seek to uncover new ideas and to share the latest research in their local areas that spark conversations in their communities. TEDx events include live speakers and recorded TED Talks, and are organized independently under a free license granted by TED. These events are not controlled by TED, but event organizers agree to abide by our format, and are offered guidelines for curation, speaker coaching, event organizing and more. They learn from us and from each other. More than 3000 events are now held annually.

TEDxMalmö

In 2019, co-founders Saga Gardevärn and Filip Landgren decided to build on their success with the first rendition of TEDxLundUniversity, by bringing thed TEDx concept to Malmö. In Sweden’s third largest city where almost 50% of the population is under the age of 35, a culture of change fills the streets and locals can be seen actively engaged in creating a better future almost every day. The theme “The Future is NOW” perfectly encapsulates the energy of Malmö today.

2020 has without a doubt been a challenging year, not only for TEDxMalmö, but for the event industry as a whole. With new COVID-19 restrictions, our team of volunteers was forced to re-think the entire event plan and start building the future of events. In December, we are finally ready to show the world what we have been working on.

We are the first-ever organisation in Sweden that will host an event in a virtual world on a virtual stage, powered by Nextech AR. We have speakers and visitors joining us from around the globe as well as local forward-thinking companies to join us in this historic event. We are proud to declare that “The Future is NOW” is not only the theme of our TEDxMalmö event, but also our way of working.

The TEDxMalmö Team

The TEDxMalmö team consists of event producers, professionals and entrepreneurs, all volunteering their precious time to organise, design and produce this extraordinary event. TEDxMalmö is incredibly proud of our international and diverse team with members from all over the world, including Europe, Asia, the Middle East, Australia and North America. Each and every one will be virtually present during the event to host, support and chat with all our visitors.
