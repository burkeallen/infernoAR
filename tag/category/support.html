Support

Have a question? Need some help? Reach out to us and we will happily assist you.

General Questions

Q: Do I have to follow the event agenda exactly as outlined?

A: Like an in-person conference, you will have a better experience if you follow the agenda as closely as possible. The program was designed to provide you with an experience as full as our in- person conference, and we encourage you to follow the program as designed. However, we know that isn’t always possible. All sessions will be available on-demand and can be watched any time after the scheduled time listed on the agenda. You will only be able to participate in Q&A sessions if you are watching in real time.

<

Q:How long may I have access to the virtual conference?

A: You may log in to the virtual conference site for 90-days post live conference. You will be able to watch sessions on-demand, visit exhibit booths, download materials, and more. We like to say, November 12th is just the beginning!

Q:Are there system or device requirements required to experience the event?

A: The conference is best experienced on a computer, but you may use almost any device to experience it. You may participate in the virtual conference from a laptop, computer (Mac or PC), smart phone or tablets (Android or iOS). You will log on to the conference from an internet browser.

Q:What internet capacity is recommended?

A: You should use a strong, wired broadband connection with a speed of at least 1.4 Mbps. If your connect is slower, you may still participate but load times may be longer than normal. Other helpful tips include: Connect to the internet using a network cable rather than using a wireless network; Disconnect from VPN or corporate networks (if possible); Close any unnecessary applications; Refrain from browsing the internet; Streaming media and/or downloading large files during this time.

Q:Can I ask speakers questions during presentations?

A: Yes, we encourage you to ask questions at any time! Engaging our presenters in Q&A is an excellent way to build engagement and to make the most of your virtual conference experience. The live General Sessions will have a Question button that can be found underneath the video player. At any time throughout these General Sessions, you may submit a question to the presenter(s). The breakout sessions will have a chat box located next to the presentation where you can interact in a live chat with the event hosts and attendees.

Q:Are chats private or public?

A: The Q&A chat in the sessions is a public feed and all participants will see all questions and answers. Chat between sponsors and attendees and between attendees are private.

Q:May I chat directly with sponsors?

A: Yes, in each sponsor’s exhibit booth, there will be a link to vendor video conferencing rooms and a chat room feature where you can connect. You encourage you to engage with sponsors during the dedicated break times and throughout the conference.
