What is the Exploration Challenge?

Safe to say that pretty much everyone is suffering from online meeting fatigue. Maintaining attention and presence suffers from "Zoom fatigue". So, while we can’t recreate the experience of an in-person event, we have created the Exploration Challenge to help make attending and exploring the Summit fun, enjoyable, educational and rewarding – and helps prevent "Zoom fatigue".

Winning:

Well, everyone wins from the education obtained and knowledge shared, the conversations had by attending the sessions, visiting the Resource Library and the sponsors in the exhibit area, and from online networking with each other! For the actual prizes, you must submit your completed entry survey that will be displayed starting at the break and closes at 5pm ET.

The Challenge works very simply:
  • Explore all the areas of the conference and find all the words of our 8-12 word phrase, that have been scattered around the Summit in the various 'rooms'. There may be more than 1 word in a room. Words may only be displayed for a few minutes, so be on the lookout!
  • Starting at the break and running through 5pm ET, a link to a survey will be displayed to all attendees for you to select what you believe the correct phrase is and submit your response. Only 1 submission per conference attendee
  • All those who respond to the survey with the correct phrase will be entered into a random drawing for 1 of 3 GREAT prizes* (see below)
  • Only those registered attendees**, who submit the correct phrase answer on the online pop-up survey will be eligible for the random drawing
  • The 3 random winners will be announced during the Conference Closing Remarks
* Prizes will be awarded in the order people are randomly selected
**Members of the TDH Board, Summit Committees, and Community Partners are not eligible to participate
