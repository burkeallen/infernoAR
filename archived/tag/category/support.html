Support

Have a question? Need some help? Reach out to us and we will happily assist you.

General Questions

Q: Will the sessions be recorded and viewable on-demand later?

A: Yes, all of the sessions will be recorded and made on-demand in the Resource Library. You can access these videos on this platform for up to 1 year after the event.

Q: How can I quickly find content on a topic of interest?

A: Have a specific topic in mind? When the on-demand videos are ready, use our Search Video box in the upper right hand corner of the platform to search on your topic. Our enhanced video search capabilities will search within each recorded video and bring you directly to points in the video related to your topic! No more wasted time fast-forwarding through sessions looking for the talking points you want to re-watch.

Q: Do I have to follow the event agenda exactly as outlined?

A: Like an in-person conference, you will have a better experience if you follow the agenda as closely as possible. The program was designed to provide you with an experience as full as our in- person conference, and we encourage you to follow the program as designed. However, we know that isn’t always possible. All sessions will be available on-demand and can be watched any time after the scheduled time listed on the agenda.

Q:How long may I have access to the virtual conference?

A: You may log in to the virtual conference site for 1 year post live conference. You will be able to watch sessions on-demand, visit exhibit booths, download materials, and more. We like to say, November 12th is just the beginning!

Q:Are there system or device requirements required to experience the event?

A: The conference is best experienced on a computer, but you may use almost any device to experience it. You may participate in the virtual conference from a laptop, computer (Mac or PC), smart phone or tablets (Android or iOS). You will log on to the conference from an internet browser.

Q:What internet capacity is recommended?

A: You should use a strong, wired broadband connection with a speed of at least 1.4 Mbps. If your connection is slower, you may still participate but load times may be longer than normal. Other helpful tips include: Connect to the internet using a network cable rather than using a wireless network; Disconnect from VPN or corporate networks (if possible); Close any unnecessary applications; Refrain from browsing the internet; Streaming media and/or downloading large files during this time.

Q:Can I ask speakers questions during presentations?

A: Due to the rapid fire nature of the event, we will have limited time for each speaker/panel during the Summit. Therefore we will not be offering Q&A with the speakers. However, every session has a live group chat box available and we encourage all attendees to get involved in the conversation through group chat during the event. We are hoping to crowd source answers to questions that may arise based on the topics that the speakers address, and all you to interact with attendees and the event hosts.

Q:Are chats private or public?

A: The group chat in the sessions is a public feed and all participants will see all comments.

Q:May I chat directly with sponsors?

A: Yes, in each sponsor’s exhibit booth, there will be a link to vendor video conferencing rooms and a chat room feature where you can connect. We encourage you to engage with sponsors during the dedicated break times and throughout the conference.

Q:Where can I find additional information about the Digital Health Leadership Summit and TAG Digital Health?

A: For additional information about the Summit, please visit our website: https://tagdigitalhealthsummit.com
To find out more about TAG Digital Health and to get involved, please visit our Society website: https://www.tagonline.org/societies/health
