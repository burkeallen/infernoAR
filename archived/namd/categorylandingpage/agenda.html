Welcome and Level Setting

Beth Kidder, NAMD Board President, will welcome Medicaid Directors and their teams to the Member Connection Series Summer Event. She will reflect on the goals of the series. Her remarks will be immediately followed by the first session.

Speaker: Beth Kidder, Florida Medicaid Director and NAMD Board President

Leading the Agency Through Times of Tumult

This session will examine how Medicaid Directors have been leading their agencies through the pandemic, recession and racial justice events. The group will describe how they are finding a path forward amidst so many unknowns. They’ll discuss how they are communicating difficult messages to staff and motivating staff during a time of remote work and periods of social isolation. Lastly, they’ll talk about how they are leading up to the Governor or legislature when communicating the strategic priorities of the agency.

Moderator: Tom Betlach, former Arizona Medicaid Director currently at Spier Consulting

Panelists

  • Ruth Johnson, Louisiana Medicaid Director
  • Jenn Jacobs, New Jersey Medicaid Director
  • Caprice Knapp, North Dakota Medicaid Director

Opportunities to Infuse Equity into Agency Operations

COVID has underscored and exacerbated existing health and health care inequities for communities of color. Medicaid Directors will share what they have been learning and doing to address the reality, including how they are incorporating an equity lens into agency operations.

Moderator: Gretchen Hammer, NAMD Senior Strategic Advisor and former Colorado Medicaid Director

Panelists

  • Raquel Hatter, Ed. D., former Commissioner of the Tennessee Dept. of Human Service and current Managing Director of the Human Services Program at The Kresge Foundation
