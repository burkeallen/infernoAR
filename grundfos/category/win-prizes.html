HOW TO WIN PRIZES

Throughout the day you will have the opportunity to accumulate prize draw entry points. There are many ways to collect these points. You already have a point for attending the Summit and the more you participate during the Summit, the more points you will receive.

  • 3 x £100 in online vouchers
  • 6 x £50 in online vouchers
  • 20 x £10
  • 10 x £5

How does it work

At the end of the day, we will tally up the number of points that each attendee has collected. Each point equates to an entry in each draw.

The draws will be made on Friday 16th October and the winners will be notified by Tuesday 20th October, via the email address used to register for the summit. We will ask you to verify your email before sending the voucher.

Thank you for joining us at the virtual summit and good luck!
