General Questions

How do I log on to the conference?

All registered attendees will receive an email with their log in information and details on how to access NAMD 2020 Virtual Connect. This email will be sent From: NAMD.live Support (noreply@infernoar.com). Please check your spam/junk folder if you don’t see the email in your Inbox.

This email will include a link to access the live conference site and your temporary login credentials. IMPORTANT: You will need to login into the site using your temporary login within 24-hours of receipt of your email to create your profile and personal password. If you do not receive this email, please email contact@nampi.net.

Do I have to follow the August 17-19 agenda exactly as outlined?

Like an in-person conference, you will have a better experience if you follow the agenda as closely as possible. The program was designed to provide you with an experience as full as our in- person conference, and we encourage you to follow the program as designed. However, we know that isn’t always possible.

All sessions will be available on-demand and can be watched any time after the scheduled time listed on the agenda. You will only be able to participate in Q&A sessions if you are watching in real time. Please see below if you have questions about how to access the sessions and view the full program.

How long may I access the virtual conference?

You may log in to the virtual conference site for 90-days post live conference. You will be able to watch sessions on-demand, visit exhibit booths, download materials, and more. We like to say, August 17-19 is just the beginning! Will I receive any materials for the conference? If you opted in for our NAMPI Virtual Conference Kit prior to the conference. Please note that all attendees who registered after August 4 will not be receiving a kit due to shipping and logistical reasons.

The printed materials included in the kit will be sent via email to attendees who did not receive the mailed kit on Thursday, August 13. If you do not receive the emailed conference kit, please contact us via email at contact@nampi.net.
